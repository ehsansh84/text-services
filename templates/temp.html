Computer Vision as a Service is an ongoing project which is designed and developed by Biomedical Modeling and Visualization Laboratory in Computer Science Department at University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, USA.
The Biomedical Modeling and Visualization Laboratory at UW-Milwaukee is established and directed by Prof. Zeyun Yu. The primary research topics in the Lab include image analysis, computer vision, geometric modeling, and scientific computing and visualization.
